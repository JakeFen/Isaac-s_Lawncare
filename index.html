About

How I got here...

My name is Isaac Fenwick and I am a student at Ashland University. In May of 2018, I decided to start Fenwick’s Services for two reasons. Number one, I love to help and interact with new people, especially those in my community. Number two, I am going to college for Business Administration and Accounting so I figured learning the ins and outs of my own business would benefit me dramatically, and the money I make in the process will help pay my college expenses. If there are any jobs from cleaning gutters to helping put up Christmas lights, do not be afraid to get into contact with me. I look forward to working with you!
